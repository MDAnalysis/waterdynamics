Welcome to waterdynamics's documentation!
=========================================================

This package provides classes and functions for the analysis of water dynamics and water interactions with other molecules in molecular simulations. The functions in this package are: water orientational relaxation, hydrogen bond lifetimes, angular distribution, mean square displacement, and survival probability.

Welcome to waterdynamics’s documentation!

This package provides classes and functions for the analysis of water dynamics and water interactions with other molecules in molecular simulations. The functions in this package are: water orientational relaxation (WOR) [1], hydrogen bond lifetimes (HBL) [2], angular distribution (AD) [3], mean square displacement (MSD) [4], and survival probability (SP) [5].

For more information about this type of analysis please refer to [6] (water in a protein cavity) and [7] (water in a nanopore).
